About Us

We are a local coffee shop located in Berkeley, California. Here at Caffeinated, we have a passion for coffee, beer, productivity, and community-building, and hope that Caffeinated is the final combination of all of these things.

A Sanctum for the Studious

As former students who used to slave away in cafes ourselves, we know the importance of having a great space to work. At Caffeinated, you'll find spacious seatings, as well as a variety of types of areas to work. Working in a group? We have large tables, round or rectangular. Studying alone together with friends? We have smaller booths and tables where you can sit across from your study-buddy. To seal the deal, we have fast, free Wi-Fi, and you can even borrow noise-canceling headphones from the cashier any time you please.

A Haven for the Hungry

We have a wide selection of locally roasted coffee, craft beer, and teas. When it comes to our homemade pastries and dishes, we only use the finest, freshest, local ingredients.

Shindigs and Soirees

Not only are we into serving top-notch food and coffee to keep you fed and awake while you get sh*t done, but we're into creating an inclusive environment conducive to community-building, people meeting people, and all-around fun. To this end, we have events held on certain days of the week, every week - visit our events page for more info!
