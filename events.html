Festivities and Functions, oh my!

Music Mondays

Are your ears in need of some sweet sounds? Come down to Caffeinated on Mondays to hear live music! We have artists of various genres coming to play every week. In the past, we have had musicians like Emancipator, Baths, Gorillaz, Lights, Dumbfoundead, Childish Gambino, Lykke Li, and STS9 play their jams at our house.

Board Game Thursdays

Are you a lover of all things strategies, shenanigans, and RNG? Come and unleash your inner competitive tactician at Caffeinated on Thursdays! We have a library of over 800 games, and are always accumulating more! We have many types of games, ranging from co-op heavy strategy games like Pandemic, word games like Codename, and party games like Exploding Kittens - it's guaranteed that you'll find something you'll love and want to play all night!

Indie Film Saturdays

Are you tired of paying $20 to watch a film that you and your friends found dumboundingly cringy after the fact? Come in and watch some of the quality films in our indie film collection, including Little Miss Sunshine, Pulp Fiction, Being John Malkovich, Lost in Translation, and Requiem for a Dream.
